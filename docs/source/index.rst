Welcome to Fish Tanks's documentation!
=======================================

**Fish Tank** is a reinforcment learning environment with the purpose
of exploring the evolution of control policy. The goal is to design a
competitive environment where agent cooperation and specilization may
be the a rewarding survival strategy. Agents will be representive cellular
life with the goal of seeking out and harvesting food resources from the environment.


Check out the :doc:`usage` section for further information, including
how to :ref:`installation` the project.

.. note::

   This project is under active development.
